### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and to stash away some info. These technologies include beacons, tags, and scripts, which help us collect and track information, as well as improve and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your device. You can totally tell your browser to refuse all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But, if you don't accept Cookies, you might miss out on some cool parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service will likely use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also hear them called clear gifs, pixel tags, or single-pixel gifs). These awesome little tools let the Company, for instance, count who's visited those pages or opened an email, and help with other website stats (like tracking how popular a section is or double-checking system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies hang out on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for giving you the services available through our Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They're key to authenticating users and stopping any sneaky, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, we simply couldn't provide the services you've asked for, and we use them just for that!

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies simply help us remember if you've given us the green light to use Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make while using the Website, like your login deets or language preference. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience, saving you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you visit!

Want more deets about the Cookies we use and your options? Just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy!
